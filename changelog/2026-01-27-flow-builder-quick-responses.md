Introducing the Flow Builder for automated conversations, quick responses for faster replies, and a major chat experience upgrade.

<!-- truncate -->

## New Features

### Mini Chatbot (Flow Builder)
Design automated message flows to handle customer queries without manual effort. Build conversation paths visually and let the chatbot guide your customers step by step.

### Quick Responses
Save your frequently used messages and send them instantly from the chat window — no more retyping the same replies.

### Group Filtering
Filter your contact lists by group to find exactly the audience you need.

## Improvements

### Chat Experience Upgrade
The chat section is now much more powerful:
- **Text formatting** — bold, italic, and more
- **Attachments** — send files and media easily
- **Template messages in open chats** — send approved templates even in active conversations

### Better Delivery & Campaign Creation
- Redesigned **campaign creation screen** for a smoother workflow
- Optimized sending logic for **higher message delivery rates**
