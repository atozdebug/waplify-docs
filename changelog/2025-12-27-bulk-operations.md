Major upgrades to how you manage contacts, groups, and campaigns — now faster and more powerful.

<!-- truncate -->

## New Features

### Bulk Group Operations
- **Create new groups** from any contact selection in one click
- **Add contacts to existing groups** directly from search or filter results
- **Select all** contacts for batch operations — no more selecting one by one

### Campaign Creation from Contact Selection
- Select contacts and **launch a campaign directly** without switching screens

### Advanced Contact Filtering
- Filter contacts by **date added**, **campaign source**, **name**, and **custom attributes**
- Combine multiple filters for precise audience targeting

### Instant Contact Export
- Export your filtered contact list as a file with a single click

## Performance Improvements

- **Faster group management** — creating and editing groups is now significantly quicker
- **Quicker bulk uploads** — large contact imports process faster
- **Optimized campaign sends** — messages go out faster and more reliably
- **Improved system responsiveness** — smoother experience across the platform
