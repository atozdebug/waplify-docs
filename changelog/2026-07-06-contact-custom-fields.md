Store the details that matter to **your** business on every contact. **Custom Fields** let you add your own fields — like City, Plan, or Lead Score — and then filter, personalize, and automate with them.

<!-- truncate -->

Standard contact details (name, phone, email, company…) are the same for everyone. Custom Fields are the extras that are unique to how you work.

## Set them up once, use them everywhere

Go to **Settings → Custom Fields** (Owners and Admins) and create up to **10** fields, each with the right type:

- **Text**, **Number**, **Date**, **Choice** (pick from a list), **Yes / No**, **Email**, or **Website**.

Once created, a field appears on **every contact** — fill it in by hand, or bring values in through [CSV import](/docs/contacts/import-contacts).

## Where custom fields pay off

- **Filter and segment** — build lists like *City is Dubai* or *Lead Score above 50*, then target them in campaigns and sequences.
- **Personalize messages** — drop a field's value into your templates and chats so each customer sees their own detail.
- **Power your automations** — read a custom field in a [Flow](/docs/flows-v2/what-is-flows-v2) to make decisions, and write to one with the [Update Contact](/docs/flows-v2/updating-contacts) block so your bot saves what it learns.

Deleting a field never wipes the values already stored on your contacts, so you can experiment safely.

See [Custom Fields](/docs/contacts/custom-fields) for the full guide.
