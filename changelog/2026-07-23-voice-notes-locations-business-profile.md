Three upgrades to the way you talk to customers: **record a voice note** right in the inbox, **send a location** they can tap to navigate to, and **edit your WhatsApp business profile** without leaving Waplify.

<!-- truncate -->

## Send a voice note

Some things are faster to say than to type. Hold the **microphone** in the message box and talk.

- You see a **live waveform** while recording, so you know it's picking you up.
- Before it sends, you get a **review bar** — play it back, and re-record or delete if you fumbled it.
- Waplify converts the recording to the format WhatsApp expects, so it arrives as a proper voice note on the customer's phone, not a file attachment.
- It appears in the chat **immediately** while it uploads, so you can carry on with the next conversation.

Great for explaining a price breakdown, reassuring an upset customer, or answering a long question in ten seconds.

## Send a location

You can now send a real map location instead of typing out an address and hoping it makes sense.

- Click the **location** option in the message box and **search for the place** — shops, landmarks, buildings — using the built-in map picker.
- The customer receives a tappable location that opens directly in their maps app with directions.
- You can also **send a location from a flow**, so a bot can share your showroom or pickup point automatically when someone asks "where are you?"

Perfect for shops, clinics, restaurants, service centres, and delivery pickups.

## Your customers see you typing

When an agent starts replying, the customer now sees the familiar **"typing…"** indicator in WhatsApp — the same cue they'd get from a friend.

It's a small thing that makes a big difference: people wait instead of sending "hello??" three more times, and the conversation feels human rather than automated.

## Edit your WhatsApp business profile

Your business profile is what customers see when they tap your name in WhatsApp. You can now manage it under **Settings**, without digging through Meta's Business Manager.

- Update your **profile photo**, **about text**, **description**, **address**, **email**, and **website**.
- Changes go live on WhatsApp straight away.
- Managing more than one number? Each has its **own profile and avatar**, shown side by side so you never edit the wrong one.

## Know how each number is connected

Every WhatsApp number in **Settings** now carries a small badge showing **how it was connected** — whether it's a full API number or one shared with the WhatsApp Business app on your phone.

That matters because the two behave differently: shared numbers let you reply from your phone and stay in sync, while API-only numbers are managed entirely from Waplify. The badge means you can tell at a glance, instead of guessing why one number behaves differently from another.
