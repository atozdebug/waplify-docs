Two upgrades that make the Waplify inbox feel effortless: you can now answer customers straight from the **WhatsApp Business app** and stay in sync, and the inbox **reconnects on its own** whenever your connection blips.

<!-- truncate -->

## Reply from your phone — everything stays in sync (coexistence)

You don't have to be at your desk to answer a customer. Reply to them directly in the **WhatsApp Business app** on your phone, and Waplify keeps up:

- **Your reply appears in the Waplify inbox** as an outgoing message — photos and files included — so your whole team sees the full conversation.
- **Unread counts stay accurate.** Read or reply on your phone and the unread badge clears in Waplify too, so the inbox doesn't pile up with chats you've already handled.
- **Your chatbot steps aside.** If a flow is running for that contact and you jump in from your phone, the bot **automatically pauses** so it never talks over you.

This is on by default; admins can control the auto-pause in [Inbox Style & Auto-Assignment](/docs/team/inbox-setup).

## A connection that heals itself

Wi-Fi drops, phones lock, tabs sleep. The inbox now handles all of it quietly:

- A calm **"Reconnecting…"** note instead of an alarming "connection lost" warning.
- It **keeps retrying on its own** — no more refreshing the page.
- The instant your network returns, or you switch back to the tab, it **reconnects immediately** and catches up on anything you missed.

See the [Inbox Overview](/docs/chat/inbox-overview) for details.
