Faster campaigns, real-time progress tracking, a new subscription dashboard, and Gmail sign-in support.

## Campaign Improvements

- **Faster speed & smoother UX** across all campaign screens
- **Real-time progress tracking** — see % completed as your campaign sends
- **Edit & duplicate campaigns** — reuse previous campaigns easily
- **Cancel scheduled campaigns** anytime before they send
- **Timezone support** — schedule campaigns in any timezone with local-time conversion
- **Improved campaign count accuracy**

## Easier Campaign Creation

- **Paste phone numbers in bulk** — no need to create a group or import contacts first
- **Variables in message headers** — personalize template headers with dynamic content

## Filtering & Data

- **Advanced filtering** inside campaign details
- **Opted-in & opted-out filtering** when selecting a group
- **Export campaign data** directly from the campaign details page

## Login & Sign Up

- **Fresh new design** for the login and sign up pages
- **Sign in with Gmail** — quick and easy one-click login

## Subscription & Billing

- **New subscription section** in Settings — track your plan status (active, upcoming, or ended)
- **Upgrade your plan** directly from the settings page
- **Download invoices** with a single tap
- **International payments** now supported

## General

- Better error handling across all flows
