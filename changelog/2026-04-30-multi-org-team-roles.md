## Invite your team

Waplify is no longer a one-person tool. Head to the new **Team** page to invite teammates by email — each person gets their own login and sets their own password when they accept.

You can resend or cancel pending invitations, and deactivate or remove members whenever you need to.

## Four roles for the right access

Every team member has a **role** that decides what they can see and do:

- **Owner** — full access, including billing
- **Admin** — manages the team, settings, and messaging — everything except billing
- **Manager** — runs campaigns, contacts, and templates
- **Agent** — handles customer chats in the inbox

Agents can also be given a **view scope** — either the whole inbox, or only the conversations assigned to them.

See [Roles & Permissions](/docs/team/roles-and-permissions) and [Managing Your Team](/docs/team/managing-your-team) for the full breakdown.

## Run multiple businesses from one account

Manage more than one business? A single Waplify login can now belong to several workspaces. Switch between them from the sidebar — and your role can be different in each one.

## Why this matters

- **Share the workload** — your whole team works from one place, no shared logins
- **Stay in control** — give each person exactly the access they need, nothing more
- **One account, every business** — switch between workspaces without logging out
