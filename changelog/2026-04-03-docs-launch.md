We are excited to launch the **Waplify Help Center** — your one-stop destination for guides, API documentation, and product updates.

<!-- truncate -->

## What's included

### Guides
Step-by-step walkthroughs for every Waplify feature, written for non-technical business owners:
- **Getting Started** — sign up, connect WhatsApp, verify your business, send your first campaign
- **Contacts & Groups** — import, manage, and organize your audience
- **Message Templates** — create and sync approved WhatsApp templates
- **Campaigns** — create, schedule, and track broadcast campaigns
- **Chat & Inbox** — real-time messaging with your customers
- **Automation** — build conversation flows with the visual flow builder
- **Team Management** — invite members and assign roles
- **Billing** — manage your subscription and payments

### API Documentation
Complete reference for the Waplify Public API:
- Send template and free-form WhatsApp messages
- Manage contacts and contact groups
- List available templates
- Receive real-time webhook notifications with signature verification
