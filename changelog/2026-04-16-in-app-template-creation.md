You can now create WhatsApp message templates directly inside Waplify — no need to jump over to the Meta platform anymore. Approved templates also show up automatically, so you no longer need to hit **Sync**.

<!-- truncate -->

## Create templates without leaving Waplify

Previously, every new template had to be created on the Meta Business platform and then synced back into Waplify. Now you can build them right inside the Waplify web app.

### Template types supported in this release

- **Limited Time Offer** — promote time-bound deals with a built-in countdown
- **Carousel** — media-card carousels (2–10 cards, each with its own image/video and buttons)

More template types will be added in upcoming releases.

## Auto-detection of approved templates

When Meta approves one of your submitted templates, it now appears in your Waplify dashboard automatically — no more clicking the **Sync** button to pull in approvals.

## Why this matters

- **Faster setup** — submit a template in a few clicks without context-switching to Meta
- **Less manual work** — approved templates show up on their own
- **One place to manage everything** — create, track, and use templates all from Waplify
