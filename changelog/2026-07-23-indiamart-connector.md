Every enquiry you get on **IndiaMART** can now become a Waplify contact automatically — no copying, no spreadsheets, no waiting. Connect it once, and new leads show up ready to message.

<!-- truncate -->

## Connect once, capture forever

Go to **Settings → Connectors**, pick **IndiaMART**, and connect your account. From that moment on, every new enquiry arrives in Waplify on its own — usually within seconds of the buyer submitting it.

Each lead becomes a proper contact, not just a row of text:

- **Name is split correctly** into first and last name, so your `{{first_name}}` greetings read naturally instead of "Hi Rajesh Kumar Sharma".
- **Phone, email, and company** are filled in and validated — bad emails are skipped rather than saved as junk.
- **The buyer's address** is saved on the contact, so you know where they're based before you reply.
- **What they asked about** — the product or enquiry detail — is stored too, so your first message can actually mention it.

## Decide where each field goes

Not every business wants the same lead detail in the same place. During setup you get a **field mapping** screen where you choose what lands where.

- Map an IndiaMART field onto a **standard contact field** (email, company, address) or onto any of your own [custom fields](/docs/contacts/custom-fields).
- Each field has a short description and tooltip, so you're not guessing what "Sender Mobile Alt" means.
- Need a custom field that doesn't exist yet? Waplify **creates it for you** as part of connecting, so you don't have to set it up first.

## Reply the moment a lead arrives

The real win is what happens next. Because IndiaMART leads become normal contacts, everything else in Waplify picks them up automatically:

- Start a [flow](/docs/flows-v2/more-ways-to-start) with the **contact created** trigger to send an instant WhatsApp welcome — while the buyer is still shopping around.
- Add the same trigger with a condition (for example, only leads for a particular product) so different enquiries get different replies.
- Enrol them in a [sequence](/docs/sequences/what-are-sequences) to follow up over the next few days without lifting a finger.

Responding within minutes instead of hours is usually the difference between winning the enquiry and losing it to the next supplier on the list.

## Good to know

- Leads only flow **in** — Waplify never changes anything on your IndiaMART account.
- Duplicate enquiries from the same buyer update the existing contact instead of creating a second one.
- You can disconnect at any time from **Settings → Connectors**; contacts already captured stay in your account.

See [Managing Contacts](/docs/contacts/managing-contacts) for what you can do with leads once they arrive.
