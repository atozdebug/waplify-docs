The Waplify inbox is now built for teams — assign conversations to teammates, hand them off, resolve them when they're done, and pick the layout that fits how you work.

<!-- truncate -->

## Pick your inbox style

Workspace owners and admins can now choose how the inbox is laid out for everyone:

- **Team** — conversations are split into **Mine**, **Unassigned**, **Open**, and **Resolved** tabs, so a group can divide the work
- **Simple** — one combined inbox, best if you mostly broadcast and reply only occasionally

You'll find this under **Settings → Inbox & routing**. See [Inbox Style & Auto-Assignment](/docs/team/inbox-setup).

## Assign, hand off, and resolve conversations

Every conversation can now be **assigned** to a specific teammate, so two people never reply to the same customer. You can:

- **Assign** an unassigned conversation to yourself or a teammate
- **Transfer** a conversation to someone else, with an optional handoff note
- **Resolve** a conversation when it's finished — and **reopen** it any time
- See a customer's details, tags, and journey in the **info panel** beside the chat

Full walkthrough: [Working as a Team in the Inbox](/docs/chat/team-inbox).

## Auto-assign new conversations

Turn on **auto-assignment** and Waplify shares new conversations across your team automatically, round-robin, so nobody has to pick them up by hand. Choose who's in the assignment pool, and decide whether reopened conversations go back to the original agent.

## More chat improvements

- **Message reactions** — react to any message with an emoji, just like in WhatsApp
- **Link previews** — links in chat now show a preview card with the page's image and title
- **A live window timer** — every conversation shows how much time is left in the 24-hour reply window

## Subscription management, refreshed

The subscription screen under **Settings** has been rebuilt — view your current plan, upgrade or schedule a downgrade, cancel, and see your full payment history, all in one place.
