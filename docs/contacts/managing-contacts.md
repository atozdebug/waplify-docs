## What is Managing Contacts?

The Contacts page is where you manage all the people you want to message on WhatsApp. You can add contacts individually, edit their details, tag them for easy filtering, and keep track of who has opted in to receive messages.

## How to use it

### Viewing your contacts

1. Go to **Contacts** from the sidebar
<!-- screenshot: Contacts page with list of contacts -->
2. You will see a table with all your contacts showing their name, phone number, tags, and opt-in status
3. Use the **search bar** to find specific contacts by name or phone number
4. Use **filters** to narrow down contacts by tags, opt-in status, or other fields

### Adding a single contact

1. On the Contacts page, click **Add Contact**
<!-- screenshot: Add Contact form -->
2. Fill in the contact details:
   - **First Name** (required)
   - **Last Name** (optional)
   - **Phone Number** with country code (required)
   - **Email** (optional)
   - **Company**, **Industry**, **Website** (optional)
3. Click **Save** to add the contact

### Editing a contact

1. Click on any contact in the list to open their details
<!-- screenshot: Contact edit panel -->
2. Update any fields you need to change
3. Click **Save** to apply the changes

### Deleting contacts

1. Select one or more contacts using the checkboxes in the table
2. Click **Delete** to remove the selected contacts
3. Confirm the deletion

:::caution
Deleting a contact is permanent. Make sure you no longer need the contact before deleting.
:::

### Exporting contacts

1. On the Contacts page, click the **Export** button
2. Your contacts will be downloaded as a CSV file (a spreadsheet format that opens in Excel or Google Sheets)
3. You can also export filtered results — apply filters first, then export

## Tips & best practices

- Use **tags** to organize contacts by category (e.g., "VIP", "New Customer", "Event Attendee") — this makes it easier to target them in campaigns
- Keep the **opt-in status** updated — only send messages to contacts who have agreed to receive them
- Regularly clean up contacts with invalid numbers to keep your quality rating high
- Use [Contact Groups](./contact-groups.md) to segment contacts for different campaign types
- For bulk additions, use the [Import Contacts](./import-contacts.md) feature instead of adding one by one

## Frequently asked questions

### Can I search for contacts by phone number?

Yes. Use the search bar on the Contacts page and type the phone number (or part of it) to find matching contacts.

### What does "opted in" mean?

Opted in means the contact has given you permission to send them WhatsApp messages. It is your responsibility to ensure you have proper consent before messaging.

### Can I undo a contact deletion?

No. Once a contact is deleted, it cannot be recovered. You would need to re-import or re-add the contact manually.

### Is there a limit on how many contacts I can have?

No. Waplify does not limit the number of contacts you can store in your account.

### Can I add custom fields to contacts?

Currently, contacts support standard fields like name, phone, email, company, industry, and website, plus tags for custom categorization.
