## What are Contact Groups?

Contact Groups let you organize your contacts into segments. For example, you might have groups like "VIP Customers", "Newsletter Subscribers", or "Event Attendees". When creating a campaign, you can target entire groups instead of selecting contacts one by one.

## How to use it

### Creating a group

1. Go to **Contact Groups** from the sidebar
2. Click **Create Group**
<!-- screenshot: Create Group dialog -->
3. Enter a **group name**
4. Click **Create** to save the group

### Adding contacts to a group

1. Open a group by clicking on it
<!-- screenshot: Group details page with contacts list -->
2. Click **Add Contacts**
3. Select contacts from your contact list and confirm
4. The selected contacts will be added to the group

### Adding new contacts directly to a group

1. Open a group
2. Click **Add New Contact**
3. Fill in the contact details — the new contact will be created and automatically added to the group

### Bulk uploading contacts to a group

1. Open a group
2. Click **Bulk Upload**
3. Upload a CSV or Excel file with your contacts
4. All contacts in the file will be added to this group
<!-- screenshot: Bulk upload within a group -->

### Removing contacts from a group

1. Open the group
2. Select the contacts you want to remove
3. Click **Remove** to take them out of the group

:::info
Removing a contact from a group does **not** delete the contact from your account. It only removes them from that specific group.
:::

### Editing or deleting a group

1. On the Contact Groups page, find the group you want to modify
2. Click **Edit** to rename the group, or **Delete** to remove it entirely
3. Deleting a group does not delete the contacts inside it

## Tips & best practices

- Create groups based on your campaign needs — for example, separate groups for different products, locations, or customer types
- A contact can belong to **multiple groups** at the same time
- Use groups when creating campaigns to easily target the right audience
- Keep group names descriptive so your team can easily find the right one
- Regularly review and clean up groups that are no longer needed
- Need to add many contacts at once? Use [Import Contacts](./import-contacts.md) to bulk upload them from a CSV or Excel file
- Once your groups are ready, head to [Creating Campaigns](../campaigns/creating-campaigns.md) to start messaging them

## Frequently asked questions

### Can a contact be in multiple groups?

Yes. A contact can belong to as many groups as you want. They will only receive one message per campaign, even if they are in multiple selected groups.

### What happens to contacts when I delete a group?

The contacts remain in your account. Deleting a group only removes the grouping — the individual contacts are not affected.

### Is there a limit on group size?

There is no limit on how many contacts can be in a single group.

### Can I create a group during bulk upload?

Yes. When you use the Bulk CSV upload feature from the Contact Groups page, you can create a new group and import contacts into it at the same time.

### How do groups work with campaigns?

When creating a campaign, you can select one or more groups as recipients. Waplify will send the message to every contact in those groups.
