## What is Business Verification?

Business verification is a process by Meta (Facebook) to confirm that your business is real and legitimate. Once verified, your WhatsApp messaging limit increases significantly — from 250 messages per day to 2,000 and beyond.

## How to use it

### What you need

- Any **one** of the following business registration documents:
  - GST Number (Goods and Services Tax registration)
  - Udyam Registration (for micro, small, and medium enterprises in India)
  - PAN Card (Permanent Account Number — a tax ID)
  - CIN Number (Corporate Identification Number — for registered companies)
  - LLPIN (Limited Liability Partnership Identification Number)
  - Or any equivalent business registration document from your country
- Your **official business name** (must match the document you upload)
- A **phone number** listed on your business document for verification (this does not need to be the same as your WhatsApp API number)
- A **running business website** (mandatory for verification)

### Step-by-step setup

1. On your Waplify dashboard, find the **Get Started** checklist
2. Click **Start Verification** on Step 3 — Meta Security Center opens in a new tab
<!-- screenshot: Meta Security Center verification page -->
3. Follow the step-by-step process to fill in your **business details**
4. Enter all details properly and **upload your business document**
<!-- screenshot: Document upload step in Meta verification -->
5. Verify using a **one-time code** (sent to your phone or email) when prompted
6. Your verification will be submitted to Meta for review
7. Come back to Waplify and click **I've started verification** to confirm

:::info
Meta states verification takes up to **48 hours**, but it usually completes sooner. You can continue with other setup steps while waiting.
:::

## Tips & best practices

- Double-check that your business name matches **exactly** with the document you upload
- Make sure your business website is live and accessible — Meta will check it during the review process
- If your document has your phone number on it, use that same number during the verification process for faster approval
- You can check the status of your verification anytime from Meta Security Center
- Start this step early — even while setting up templates and contacts — since it involves a waiting period

## Frequently asked questions

### Why do I need to verify my business?

Meta limits unverified businesses to **250 messages per day**. After verification, your limit increases to **2,000 messages per day** and grows automatically as you maintain good quality.

### What if my verification is rejected?

If Meta rejects your verification, check the reason provided and resubmit with corrected information. Common issues include mismatched business names or unreadable documents.

### Can I send messages while verification is pending?

Yes! You can still send messages with the default limit of 250 per day while your verification is being reviewed.

### How long does verification take?

Meta says up to 48 hours, but most verifications complete within a few hours to one business day.

### Do I need to verify again if I add a new phone number?

No. Business verification is done at the Meta Business Portfolio level, not per phone number. Once verified, all phone numbers under that business benefit from higher limits.
