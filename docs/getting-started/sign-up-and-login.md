## What is Sign Up & Log In?

Waplify lets you create an account using your email or Google account. Once signed up, you can log in anytime to manage your WhatsApp campaigns, contacts, and more.

## How to use it

### Create a new account

1. Go to [app.waplify.com](https://app.waplify.com) and click **Sign Up**
<!-- screenshot: Sign up page with email and Google options -->
2. Enter your **full name**, **email address**, and create a **password**
3. You will receive a **verification code** on your email — enter it to verify your account
4. Once verified, you are logged in and ready to start the setup wizard

### Sign up with Google

1. On the sign-up page, click **Continue with Google**
2. Select your Google account
3. If this is your first time, you will be asked to enter your **phone number** and **name** to complete setup
<!-- screenshot: Complete signup form for Google users -->

### Log in to your account

1. Go to [app.waplify.com](https://app.waplify.com) and click **Log In**
2. Enter your **email** and **password**, then click **Log In**
3. Or click **Continue with Google** to log in with your Google account

### Reset your password

1. On the login page, click **Forgot Password?**
2. Enter your email address and click **Send Code**
3. Enter the verification code sent to your email
4. Set a new password and click **Reset Password**
<!-- screenshot: Forgot password page -->

## Tips & best practices

- Use a strong password with at least 8 characters
- If you sign up with Google, you can still set a password later from your account settings
- If you manage multiple businesses, Waplify supports multiple organizations — you can switch between them from the sidebar
- Keep your email address up to date so you can always recover your account

## Frequently asked questions

### Can I use the same email for multiple accounts?

No, each email address can only be linked to one Waplify account. However, you can be a member of multiple organizations with the same account.

### What if I don't receive the verification email?

Check your spam or junk folder first. If you still don't see it, try again after a few minutes. Make sure you entered the correct email address.

### Can I change my email address later?

You can update your profile information from the **Settings** page after logging in.

### Is my data secure?

Yes. Waplify uses encrypted connections and secure authentication to protect your account and data.

### Can I invite my team members?

Yes! Once your account is set up, you can invite team members from the **Team** section. They will receive an email invitation to join your organization.
