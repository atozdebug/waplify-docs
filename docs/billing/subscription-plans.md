## What are Subscription Plans?

Waplify offers different subscription plans to access the platform's features. Your subscription covers the Waplify platform itself — this is separate from WhatsApp messaging costs, which are charged by Meta directly.

## How to use it

### Viewing available plans

1. Go to **Settings > Subscription** from the sidebar
<!-- screenshot: Subscription page showing available plans -->
2. You will see the available plans with their pricing and features:
   - **Quarterly** — pay every 3 months
   - **Yearly** — pay annually (best value)
   - **Lifetime** — one-time payment for permanent access

### Subscribing to a plan

1. On the Subscription page, select the plan you want
2. Click **Subscribe** or **Upgrade**
3. The Razorpay payment window will open
<!-- screenshot: Razorpay payment window -->
4. Enter your payment details and complete the payment
5. Your plan will be activated immediately

### Applying a coupon code

1. During checkout, look for the **coupon code** field
2. Enter your code and click **Apply**
3. The discount will be reflected in the final price
<!-- screenshot: Coupon code field during checkout -->

### Upgrading your plan

1. Go to **Settings > Subscription**
2. Select a higher plan
3. Complete the payment — the upgrade takes effect immediately

### Downgrading your plan

1. Go to **Settings > Subscription**
2. Select a lower plan
3. The downgrade will be **scheduled** to take effect at the end of your current billing period
4. You can cancel the scheduled downgrade before it takes effect

### Cancelling your subscription

1. Go to **Settings > Subscription**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Your access will continue until the end of the current billing period
4. After that, your account will move to an expired state

### Viewing payment history

1. Go to **Settings > Subscription**
2. Scroll down to the **Payment History** section
3. You will see all past payments with amounts, dates, and statuses
<!-- screenshot: Payment history table -->

## Tips & best practices

- **Yearly plans** offer the best value — you save significantly compared to quarterly billing
- Your Waplify subscription and Meta messaging costs are **separate charges**:
  - **Waplify subscription** = platform access (paid to Waplify)
  - **Messaging costs** = per-conversation charges (paid to Meta)
- Keep your [payment method](../getting-started/add-payment-method.md) up to date to avoid service interruption
- If your plan expires, your data is safe — you just cannot access the platform until you renew
- Check if any **coupon codes** are available before subscribing to save money

## Frequently asked questions

### What happens when my trial ends?

After the trial period, you will need to subscribe to a paid plan to continue using Waplify. Your data (contacts, campaigns, templates) will be preserved.

### What happens if my subscription expires?

There is a short **grace period** after expiration where you can still access your account. After the grace period, you will need to renew your subscription. Your data is never deleted.

### Can I get a refund?

Refund policies depend on the specific plan and circumstances. Contact support at support@waplify.io for refund requests.

### Is there a free plan?

Waplify offers a trial period for new accounts. After the trial, a paid subscription is required.

### What payment methods are accepted?

Waplify uses Razorpay for payments, which supports credit cards, debit cards, UPI, net banking, and other payment methods available in your region. You can also review your past transactions on the [Payments & Invoices](./payments-and-invoices.md) page.
