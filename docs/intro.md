# Welcome to Waplify

Waplify is a WhatsApp marketing and automation platform built for small businesses. It helps you connect with your customers on WhatsApp — send broadcasts, automate replies, manage contacts, and track results — all from one dashboard.

## What can you do with Waplify?

- **Send WhatsApp Broadcasts** — Reach thousands of customers with personalized messages
- **Automate Conversations** — Set up chatbots and auto-replies that work 24/7
- **Manage Contacts** — Import, organize, and segment your audience
- **Create Templates** — Build WhatsApp-approved message templates
- **Track Performance** — See delivery, read, and response rates for every campaign
- **Collaborate with your Team** — Share a team inbox so no message gets missed

## Getting Started

New to Waplify? Follow these steps to get up and running:

1. [**Sign up & log in**](./getting-started/sign-up-and-login.md) — create your account with email or Google
2. [**Connect your WhatsApp number**](./getting-started/connect-whatsapp.md) — link your number through Meta's WhatsApp Business API
3. [**Add a payment method on Meta**](./getting-started/add-payment-method.md) — set up billing for WhatsApp messaging costs
4. [**Verify your business**](./getting-started/verify-your-business.md) — unlock higher messaging limits
5. [**Import your contacts**](./contacts/import-contacts.md) — bulk upload from CSV or Excel
6. [**Create a message template**](./templates/message-templates.md) — get an approved template ready
7. [**Send your first campaign!**](./getting-started/your-first-campaign.md) — a complete end-to-end walkthrough
